Our Paper Projects

PAPER FOR INDUSTY PACKAGE

Industrial paper is our main industry, we provide packaging and multi-purpose paper.

BAG-MAKING PAPER

Paper bags are also one of our main businesses. It is a pleasure and luxury to make bags with 100% recycled paper.

SPECIAL MAKING FOR CLIENTS

For customers who have special requirements, we can customize them according to their needs.

CREP PEODUCTION

We can provide on-site recycling services for customers and return the finished products to customers.

The paper protection project is our latest service. Recycling and remanufacturing paper is our mission.
